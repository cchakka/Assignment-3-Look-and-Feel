FIND THE RIGHT FIT

The Department of Education fosters educational excellence, and to ensures equal access to educational opportunity for all.

Our mission is to promote student achievement and preparation for global competitiveness by fostering educational excellence and ensuring equal access.

In the United States, there are around 3,700 institutions. How do you find the best match for you when there are so many options? A college tracker and search engine is the best place to begin. You may narrow down your search results by region and other variables.

At any point of your college career, research colleges, apply for scholarships, and figure out where you fit.
